TrackApp is now live for all iOS devices

TrackApp is a clean, simple workout tracking tool that gives you the power to track your workouts however you want!

With its minimalist design you can track all of your workouts no matter the type: running, swimming, strength training, HIT, and CrossFit are all easily trackable within TrackApp.

The key to the success of TrackApp is for you to keep your tracking consistent. With minimal input fields you are not overwhelmed with exercise types, and various configurations. Track the values you want within a given field and the progression tracker does the rest. You'll able to easily compare exercises done across multiple sessions, to help gauge progression and determine what volume should be done in your next session.

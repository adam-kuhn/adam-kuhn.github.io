Frequently asked Questions

  1. What is the difference between TrackApp and TrackApp-Basic?

    TrackApp is the complete TrackApp version with no limitations. TrackApp-Basic is the trial version of TrackApp. TrackApp-Basic allows gives you access to most TrackApp features, and allows you to log 5 sessions. Once the 5 sessions have been logged you will need to purchase TrackApp to continue logging your progress.

  2. Are there any subscriptions or in-app purchases?

    No! There are no additional costs to TrackApp. A small one time payment is all you need to do to download and use the app.

  3. Are new features coming?

    Yes! TrackApp is under active development and continually pushes new features.

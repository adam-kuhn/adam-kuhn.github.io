TrackApp Privacy Policy

Thank you for using TrackApp!

This Privacy Policy describes, the how your data is used when you use the Service (TrackApp)

If you do not agree with the terms of this policy please discontinue use of our application. You can delete your account and all associated data via the application

Collected Data

Personal Data

You are required to provide your email upon registering to the application. Before registration is complete you will be required to verify you have access to the provided email. In the event of a forgotten password, our application is able to send you a reset code to the provided email. If access is lost to your email we are unable to transfer accounts or change emails to do security reasons.

Workout Data

Our application is a workout tracking app, and all data entered is considered to be your workout data. This data will not be exposed to third parties. We may use this data to gauge usage, and help develop new and refine old features.

Data Retention

We retain the right to retain your data indefinitely, for as long as our application and its servers are running

Deleting your Account

You can delete your account at any time via the application

  1. Open the application
  2. Go to your profile
  3. Press "Delete Account
  4. Confirm account deletion

By following the above steps your account and all associated data will be removed

Children's Privacy

Our application does not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from anyone under the age of 13. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal data, please contact us. If we become aware that we have collected personal data from anyone under the age of 13 without verification of parental consent, we take steps to remove that information from our servers.

Changes to this Policy

This policy replaces all previous policies. We reserve the right to change this policy at any time.
